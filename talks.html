Talks

Saint Louis Lambda Lounge Lightning Talk
December 2, 2010

In this talk, given to the Saint Louis Lambda Lounge, Michael Schade quickly discusses the background, approach, and technical implementation of the Accentuate.us system, and then demonstrates the new vim plugin, unreleased Apple Mac OS X service, and the just-released 0.9 version of the Firefox add-on.

Saint Louis University (SLU) Math/CS Club
November 10, 2010

In this talk, given to the SLU Math/CS club, Kevin Scannell and Michael Schade discuss the background and structure of the Accentuate.us system, including a demonstration of the new Firefox add-on and the unreleased Apple Mac OS X service.
