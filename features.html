Features

  • Multilingual — Restore special characters in 116 Languages.
  • Web Ready — Correct what you type anywhere on the web with the Accentuate.us Firefox add-on.
  • Always Improving — Accentuate.us becomes more accurate as we receive feedback provided by users around the world.
  • Translatable — The Firefox add-on is fully translatable. Contact us if you'd like to translate the menus into your own language.
  • Free — Accentuate.us is free and open source software, available under the GNU General Public License (GPLv3) on SourceForge and GitHub.
  • Easy Install — The Firefox add-on is lightweight, quick to download, and easy to install. Just a couple clicks, a restart of Firefox, and you're up and running!
  • Secure — We care about your privacy. The texts you restore are not stored on our servers unless you choose to provide them as feedback, in which case you remain anonymous.
